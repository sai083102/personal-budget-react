Personal Budget

A personal-budget management app

Stay on track

Do you know where you are spending your money? If you really stop to track it down, you would be surprised! Proper budget management depends on real data, and this app will help you with that!

Alerts

What if your clothing budget ran out? You will get an alert. The goal is to never go over the budget.

Results

People who stick to a financial plan, budgeting every expense, get out of debt faster! They also live happier lives because they can spend without guilt or fear, knowing that everything is accounted for.

Free

This app is free! And you are the only one holding your data!
